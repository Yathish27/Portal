Frequently Asked Questions

Which pricing plan is right for me?
We understand that each organization is unique, requiring specific features to support its workflows and projects. Above you can see the features included in the different plans to support your needs. If you need help in choosing the right plan for you, reach out to our sales team.
How does Plurall AI detect fraud and deepfakes?
Our AI models utilize advanced machine learning algorithms, including neural networks trained on vast datasets of both genuine and manipulated content. For fraud detection, we analyze behavioral patterns, voice biometrics, and contextual cues. For deepfakes, we look for subtle inconsistencies in visual and auditory data that are undetectable to the human eye or ear.
What types of businesses can benefit from Plurall AI?
Plurall AI is designed to benefit a wide range of industries, including finance, insurance, e-commerce, customer service, and social media platforms. Any business that deals with user-generated content, online transactions, or relies on secure digital interactions can leverage our technology to enhance security and build trust.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my subscription plan?
Yes, you can easily upgrade or downgrade your subscription plan at any time through your account settings. Changes will take effect immediately, and your billing will be adjusted proportionally.
Is there a free trial available?
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for new users to explore the basic features of Plurall AI. No credit card is required to start your trial.
Are there any additional fees beyond the subscription cost?
Our subscription plans are designed to be all-inclusive for the features and usage tiers specified. However, custom integrations, enterprise-level support, or exceeding high usage limits may incur additional, pre-agreed-upon fees.
How can I manage my billing?
You can manage all billing-related aspects, including payment methods, invoices, and subscription details, directly from the 'Settings' section of your Plurall AI dashboard under 'Billing & Plans'.
Are there limits on API calls?
API call limits vary depending on your chosen subscription plan. Our basic plans include a generous allowance, and higher-tier plans offer significantly increased limits. Custom enterprise solutions can be tailored to meet specific high-volume requirements. You can monitor your current usage in the 'API Usage' section of your dashboard.
How does Plurall AI handle user data?
We prioritize data privacy and security. Plurall AI adheres to strict data protection regulations, including GDPR and CCPA. All data is anonymized and encrypted where appropriate, and we never share or sell user data to third parties. Our full privacy policy is available on our website.
What measures are in place to ensure data security?
Plurall AI employs industry-leading security practices, including end-to-end encryption, multi-factor authentication, regular security audits, and compliance with ISO 27001 standards. Our infrastructure is hosted on secure cloud platforms with robust physical and network security measures.
